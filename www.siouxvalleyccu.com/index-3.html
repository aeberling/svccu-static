Sioux Valley Community Credit Union Promotions

Welcome

Sioux Valley Community Credit Union has been open since 1952. The credit union has assets totaling $28.46 Million and provides banking services to more than 3,000 members.

What's New

We offer IDA Accounts

Sioux Valley Community Credit Union is now offering IDA Accounts (Individual Development Account). This is to help you save and achieve your goals such as owning your first vehicle, owning a home, expanding your small business, and help with paying for your education.

Watch Out For Scams

Sioux Valley Community Credit Union wants to remind our members to watch out for scams this Holiday season. Sioux Valley does not support or recommend using Zelle pay or any other cash transfer app to purchase anything. Zelle recommends to use with family and friends only.

Sioux Valley MasterCard

Sioux Valley Community Credit Union’s MasterCard is accepted throughout the United States and is designed with your financial needs in mind.You may apply for a “line-of-credit” up to $5,000.00 and can be used at any retailer, merchant, or service provider that displays the MasterCard ® logo.

Matched Rates

Sioux Valley can match a competitor's auto loan rate to as low as 1.90% APR. Speak to a Loan Officer today!
