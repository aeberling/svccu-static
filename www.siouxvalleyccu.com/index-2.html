Sioux Valley Community Credit Union Promotions

Welcome

Sioux Valley Community Credit Union has been open since 1952. The credit union has assets totaling $38 Million and provides banking services to more than 3,500 members.

What's New

New Services!!

Members can now order checks from our website! Just click the Check Order tab on the left side of the Home Page. By clicking this tab you will be taken to the Main Street link for your check reorder. The ABA or routing number is populated for you with the correct Sioux Valley number. You will need to input your account number as it appears on your checks (exclude the 0’s in front of your number). Once you select submit you will be able to verify the information on your check and proceed with making any changes and ordering your checks!

Sioux Valley members who have our Mastercard credit card can now access their credit card activity and statements via our On-line Banking. Simply log into your on-line banking from our website and click on the gray area of your credit card tab and you will be taken to your Mastercard information!

Watch Out For Scams

Sioux Valley Community Credit Union wants to remind our members to watch out for scams this Holiday season. Sioux Valley does not support or recommend using Zelle pay or any other cash transfer app to purchase anything. Zelle recommends to use with family and friends only.

Sioux Valley MasterCard

Sioux Valley Community Credit Union’s MasterCard is accepted throughout the United States and is designed with your financial needs in mind.You may apply for a “line-of-credit” up to $5,000.00 and can be used at any retailer, merchant, or service provider that displays the MasterCard ® logo.
